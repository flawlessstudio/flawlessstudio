# Archetype 14 — Content / Media / Publishing

> **Flawless Method** | `docs/archetypes/` | v1.0.0 | 2026-05-25

---

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Output type** | Content programme, media production, or publishing operation |
| **Novelty class** | Mark n — format and channel known; editorial direction, volume, and cadence variable |
| **Primary standard** | Content strategy (Halvorson), editorial standards, platform-specific guidelines |
| **Sector** | Media, Publishing, Marketing, Education, Corporate Comms, Creator Economy |

---

## Scope

All activities from content strategy and editorial planning through production, distribution, performance measurement, and optimisation. Covers written, visual, audio, and video content across owned, earned, and paid channels.

---

## Exclusions

| Out of scope | Redirect to |
|---|---|
| Brand identity design | Archetype 13 |
| Software product for content delivery | Archetype 01 |
| Event production as primary output | Archetype 15 |
| Regulatory compliance communications only | Archetype 11 |

---

## Primary Use Cases

- Editorial content programme (blog, newsletter, podcast)
- Video production series (YouTube, social, corporate)
- Content marketing strategy and execution
- Book, report, or long-form publication
- AI-assisted content operation at scale

---

## Phase Structure

| # | Phase | Key outputs |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Strategy** | Content audit, audience definition, editorial mission, channel strategy |
| 2 | **Planning** | Editorial calendar, content types, production workflow, distribution plan |
| 3 | **Production** | Content briefs, drafts, review cycles, final assets |
| 4 | **Distribution** | Publishing schedule, SEO/metadata, channel-specific formatting |
| 5 | **Measurement** | KPI dashboard, analytics setup, A/B testing framework |
| 6 | **Optimisation** | Performance review cycle, content updates, sunset process for outdated content |

---

## Quality Controls

- Editorial mission and audience definition approved before production begins
- Style guide and voice/tone guidelines published before first content goes live
- Every content piece has a defined owner, brief, and review cycle
- SEO/metadata checklist applied to all indexable content
- Fact-checking process documented for factual or regulated content
- Accessibility standards applied (alt text, captions, readability score)
- Sunset date or review trigger assigned to evergreen content

---

## Validation Gates

| Gate | Criteria | Who approves |
|---|---|---|
| **G1 — Strategy** | Editorial mission approved, audience validated, channels agreed | Content Lead + Sponsor |
| **G2 — Planning** | Editorial calendar for first 90 days approved, workflow agreed | Content Lead + Editor |
| **G3 — Production pilot** | First 5 pieces produced, reviewed, and published to standard | Editor + Content Lead |
| **G4 — Distribution live** | All channels live, analytics tracking verified, SEO baseline set | Content Lead + Analytics |
| **G5 — Optimisation cycle** | First performance review completed, backlog prioritised | Content Lead + Sponsor |

---

## Resilience & Recovery

- Content backup and version control for all published assets
- Editorial escalation path for sensitive, controversial, or regulated content
- Crisis communications protocol for content withdrawal or correction
- Platform dependency risk plan (algorithm change, platform shutdown)
- IP and licensing audit for all third-party content used

---

## International Validity

| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| WCAG 2.2 AA | Accessibility for digital content |
| GDPR / CCPA | Data collection via content (email capture, analytics) |
| FTC / ASA guidelines | Sponsored content and advertising disclosure |
| ISO 639 | Language and locale standards |
| Schema.org / OpenGraph | Structured data and social metadata |
| COPPA | Content targeting children (US) |

---

## Lock Condition

**Locks when:** Content operation is in steady-state production, optimisation cycle active, KPIs met for 60 days, and governance owner confirmed.

**Reopens when:** Audience shift detected, channel strategy change, brand repositioning, or platform migration required.
