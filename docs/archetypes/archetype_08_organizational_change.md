# Archetype 08 — Organisational Change

> **Flawless Method** | `docs/archetypes/` | v1.0.0 | 2026-05-24

---

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Output type** | Changed operating model, organisational structure, culture, or capability |
| **Novelty class** | Mark 1 — target state emergent; adoption path non-linear |
| **Primary standard** | Prosci ADKAR, Kotter 8-Step, PMBOK 7, McKinsey 7S |
| **Sector** | Corporate, Government, Public Sector, Healthcare, Education, NGO |

---

## Scope

All activities from change diagnosis through vision design, communication, engagement, adoption, embedding, and sustainability measurement. Covers structural, process, cultural, and technology-driven change programmes.

---

## Exclusions

| Out of scope | Redirect to |
|---|---|
| Known solution deployment with no adoption risk | Archetype 09 |
| Portfolio or programme governance only | Archetype 18 |
| Training programme as a standalone output | Archetype 17 |
| Regulatory compliance programme | Archetype 11 |

---

## Primary Use Cases

- Merger and acquisition integration
- Digital transformation adoption programme
- Operating model redesign
- Cultural transformation or values programme
- Post-restructuring stabilisation

---

## Phase Structure

| # | Phase | Key outputs |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Diagnosis** | Change readiness assessment, impact analysis, stakeholder map |
| 2 | **Vision** | Change vision, case for change, sponsor alignment |
| 3 | **Design** | Change plan, communication strategy, resistance management plan |
| 4 | **Communication** | Communications deployed, Q&A channels active, leadership visible |
| 5 | **Adoption** | Training delivered, coaching active, adoption KPIs tracked |
| 6 | **Embedding** | Change in BAU, behaviours measured, sustainability plan active |

---

## Quality Controls

- Change readiness score measured before launch
- Stakeholder engagement matrix maintained and updated weekly
- Adoption KPIs defined and baselined before Phase 4
- Resistance log maintained with owner and mitigation per item
- Sponsor visibility score tracked throughout
- Benefits realisation plan linked to change objectives
- Post-change pulse survey scheduled at 30, 60, 90 days

---

## Validation Gates

| Gate | Criteria | Who approves |
|---|---|---|
| **G1 — Sponsor alignment** | Executive sponsor committed, resources allocated, mandate clear | Sponsor + HR Lead |
| **G2 — Design approved** | Change plan and communication strategy signed off | Sponsor + Change Lead |
| **G3 — Adoption milestone** | Adoption KPI ≥ target at midpoint, resistance below threshold | Change Lead + Sponsor |
| **G4 — Embedding review** | Change measured in BAU, benefits baseline confirmed | Change Lead + CFO/COO |

---

## Resilience & Recovery

- Rollback plan for reversible structural or process changes
- Escalation path for critical resistance or sponsor disengagement
- Contingency plan for loss of key change agents
- Regular pulse surveys to detect adoption regression
- Communications refresh cycle if engagement drops

---

## International Validity

| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| Prosci ADKAR | Individual change adoption model |
| Kotter 8-Step | Organisational change leadership |
| McKinsey 7S | Organisational alignment framework |
| PMBOK 7th Ed. | Project management and benefits realisation |
| ISO 30414:2018 | Human capital reporting |
| ACMP Standard for CM | Change management professional standard |

---

## Lock Condition

**Locks when:** Change is embedded in BAU, adoption KPIs sustained for 90 days, and benefits baseline confirmed.

**Reopens when:** Adoption regression detected, restructuring reversed, merger fails, or new regulatory requirement triggers follow-on change.
