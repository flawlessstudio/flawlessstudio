# Archetype 11 — Regulatory / Compliance

> **Flawless Method** | `docs/archetypes/` | v1.0.0 | 2026-05-25

---

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Output type** | Implemented compliance programme, regulatory approval, or certified management system |
| **Novelty class** | Mark n — regulatory framework known; gap, remediation path, and evidence variable |
| **Primary standard** | ISO 19600, ISO 37301:2021, GDPR, sector-specific regulation |
| **Sector** | Finance, Healthcare, Pharma, Food, Energy, Tech, Public Sector, Any regulated industry |

---

## Scope

All activities from regulatory gap assessment through policy design, control implementation, training, audit, remediation, and certification or regulatory approval. Covers legal compliance, industry standards, and internal governance frameworks.

---

## Exclusions

| Out of scope | Redirect to |
|---|---|
| IT infrastructure security only | Archetype 10 |
| Quality management for manufacturing | Archetype 06 |
| Organisational change driven by compliance | Archetype 08 |
| Legal dispute or litigation | External legal counsel |

---

## Primary Use Cases

- GDPR / data protection compliance programme
- ISO 27001 or ISO 9001 certification
- Financial services regulatory compliance (MiFID II, PSD2, Basel III)
- Pharmaceutical GMP / GCP compliance
- ESG / sustainability reporting framework (CSRD, GRI, TCFD)

---

## Phase Structure

| # | Phase | Key outputs |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Gap assessment** | Regulatory inventory, gap analysis, risk register, remediation roadmap |
| 2 | **Policy design** | Policies, procedures, controls matrix, accountability framework |
| 3 | **Implementation** | Controls deployed, systems configured, training delivered |
| 4 | **Internal audit** | Audit plan, findings log, CAPA register |
| 5 | **Certification / approval** | External audit, regulator submission, certificate or approval received |
| 6 | **Ongoing monitoring** | KRI/KPI dashboard, periodic review cycle, regulatory change tracking |

---

## Quality Controls

- Regulatory inventory complete and owner-assigned per requirement
- Gap analysis validated by qualified compliance officer or external counsel
- Controls matrix maps each requirement to at least one control with evidence type
- Training completion rate ≥ 95% before certification audit
- CAPA register active with no unresolved critical findings before external audit
- Regulatory change monitoring process documented and tested
- Evidence retention policy aligned to regulatory retention requirements

---

## Validation Gates

| Gate | Criteria | Who approves |
|---|---|---|
| **G1 — Gap assessment** | Regulatory inventory complete, gap analysis signed off | Compliance Lead + Legal |
| **G2 — Controls design** | Controls matrix approved, policies published | Compliance Lead + CISO/DPO |
| **G3 — Implementation** | Controls operational, training complete, evidence collected | Compliance Lead + Internal Audit |
| **G4 — Internal audit** | No critical findings unresolved, CAPA plan approved | Internal Audit + Board/Exec |
| **G5 — Certification** | External audit passed or regulatory approval received | Compliance Lead + Regulator |

---

## Resilience & Recovery

- Regulatory breach response plan documented and tested
- Escalation path to legal counsel and regulator defined
- Whistleblower / speak-up channel active and independent
- Periodic regulatory horizon scanning to detect upcoming changes
- Business continuity plan for critical compliance functions

---

## International Validity

| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| ISO 37301:2021 | Compliance management systems |
| ISO 19600:2014 | Compliance management guidelines |
| GDPR / CCPA | Data protection and privacy |
| ISO 27001:2022 | Information security management |
| ISO 9001:2015 | Quality management |
| CSRD / GRI / TCFD | ESG and sustainability reporting |
| SOX | Financial controls (US-listed companies) |
| Basel III / MiFID II | Financial services regulation |

---

## Lock Condition

**Locks when:** Certification or regulatory approval received, ongoing monitoring cycle active, and no critical CAPAs open.

**Reopens when:** Regulatory change requires gap re-assessment, surveillance audit finding, certification renewal cycle, or material business change triggers compliance review.
