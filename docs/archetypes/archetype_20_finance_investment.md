# Archetype 20 — Finance / Investment

> **Flawless Method** | `docs/archetypes/` | v1.0.0 | 2026-05-25

---

## Identity

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Output type** | Financial model, investment decision, fund, or financial product |
| **Novelty class** | Mark n — financial objective known; structure, instrument, and market variable |
| **Primary standard** | CFA Institute standards, IFRS / GAAP, Basel III, MiFID II, ISO 31000 |
| **Sector** | Finance, Investment, Venture Capital, Corporate Finance, FinTech, Real Estate |

---

## Scope

All activities from financial analysis and modelling through investment structuring, due diligence, execution, and performance monitoring. Covers corporate finance, venture investment, fund management, financial product design, and treasury operations.

---

## Exclusions

| Out of scope | Redirect to |
|---|---|
| Regulatory compliance programme | Archetype 11 |
| FinTech software product | Archetype 01 |
| Organisational restructuring with financial component | Archetype 08 |
| Data analytics for finance (no investment decision) | Archetype 16 |

---

## Primary Use Cases

- Startup fundraising (pre-seed to Series B)
- M&A transaction and due diligence
- Fund design and launch (VC, PE, hedge)
- Corporate financial modelling and budgeting
- Financial product design (structured product, ETF, loan)

---

## Phase Structure

| # | Phase | Key outputs |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Analysis** | Financial model, market analysis, valuation, investment thesis |
| 2 | **Structuring** | Deal structure, term sheet, legal framework, risk matrix |
| 3 | **Due diligence** | Financial DD, legal DD, operational DD, risk assessment |
| 4 | **Execution** | Transaction closed, capital deployed, legal docs signed |
| 5 | **Monitoring** | Portfolio / financial KPI tracking, board reporting, covenant monitoring |
| 6 | **Exit / closure** | Exit strategy executed or financial product matured, returns documented |

---

## Quality Controls

- Financial model peer-reviewed by independent analyst before any investment decision
- Sensitivity analysis covering ≥ 3 scenarios (base, bull, bear) before execution
- All assumptions documented with source and date
- Legal due diligence complete before transaction execution
- Conflict of interest declaration completed by all decision-makers
- Investment committee or board approval obtained before capital deployment
- Regulatory requirements confirmed (MiFID II, prospectus, KYC/AML) before launch

---

## Validation Gates

| Gate | Criteria | Who approves |
|---|---|---|
| **G1 — Analysis** | Financial model reviewed, valuation agreed, thesis documented | Investment Lead + CFO |
| **G2 — Structuring** | Term sheet agreed, legal framework confirmed | Investment Lead + Legal + CFO |
| **G3 — Due diligence** | All DD workstreams complete, no blockers | Investment Committee |
| **G4 — Execution** | Investment committee approval, legal docs signed | Board / Investment Committee |
| **G5 — Monitoring** | KPIs tracked, reporting cadence active, covenants monitored | Investment Lead + CFO |

---

## Resilience & Recovery

- Stress test model against adverse market scenarios before execution
- Liquidity buffer policy defined for fund or treasury operations
- Covenant breach escalation procedure documented
- Legal dispute resolution mechanism defined in all transaction documents
- Regulatory change monitoring active for all regulated financial products

---

## International Validity

| Standard | Scope |
|---|---|
| IFRS / US GAAP | Financial reporting standards |
| CFA Institute Standards | Investment analysis and ethics |
| Basel III | Banking capital and liquidity requirements |
| MiFID II | Investment services regulation (EU) |
| ISO 31000:2018 | Risk management |
| ILPA Principles | Private equity fund governance |
| AIFMD | Alternative investment fund managers (EU) |

---

## Lock Condition

**Locks when:** Transaction closed or financial product launched, monitoring active, and all legal obligations fulfilled.

**Reopens when:** Exit event, covenant breach, regulatory change, portfolio company material event, or fund rebalancing trigger.
