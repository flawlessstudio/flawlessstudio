# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project applies a single standard: **professional quality in every interaction**.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to:

- Communicate with precision and respect
- Provide feedback that is specific, actionable, and constructive
- Accept critique of work without personalizing it
- Prioritize the quality of the output over the comfort of the process

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment of any kind
- Deliberately vague or unhelpful feedback
- Ignoring established canon without formal justification

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to `oneflawlessstudio@gmail.com`. All reports are reviewed by the maintainer and handled with discretion.

Violations may result in removal of contributions or loss of participation rights.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public representation of the project.
